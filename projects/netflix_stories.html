Netflix Stories



Netflix Stories is an interactive mobile game included with a Netflix subscription where players experience visual-novel, choose-your-own-adventure gameplay. Players customize their character, read through dialogue-driven scenes, and make choices that influence relationships and story outcomes in stories based on Netflix shows like Emily in Paris, Outer Banks, Love Is Blind, Selling Sunset, Perfect Match, Virgin River, and Sex Education.
