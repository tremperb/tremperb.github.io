Dance Of Warriors



About

Dance of Warriors is a 3D game currently being developed in Unity3D by myself, Cameron Markwell, Gauge Hartwell, and Aaron Koffel as part of our senior project at Oregon State University. This project seeks to explore the relationship between a human player and enemy AI. Specifically, how to evoke a respective feeling of dancing between these two characters. To accomplish this, the game focuses on the idea of music rhythms such as on beat actions or attacks, telegraphs which limit movement according to our given song, and shifting which character can be on offense or defense.
