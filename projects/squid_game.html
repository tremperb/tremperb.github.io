Squid Game: Unleashed



About

Squid Game: Unleashed is a mobile game adaptation inspired by the global hit series. The game combines high-stakes challenges, competitive gameplay, and dynamic environments, allowing players to experience the intensity and strategy of the show. Launched on Netflix Games, the title achieved over 20 million downloads worldwide.


My Contributions

As a Senior Software Engineer on the social features team, I designed, implemented, and maintained social systems, such as the in-game chat. I led technical design discussions, mentored fellow engineers, and established UI standards to ensure consistency, polish, and scalability. I also supported live operations, handling updates and troubleshooting issues to maintain a seamless player experience.

Technical Highlights

  • Built social gameplay systems, including the in-game chat, from technical design to live support.
  • Developed and enforced UI standards, mentoring engineers to maintain consistency across features.
  • Implemented reusable UI frameworks and editor tools to streamline designer workflows.
  • Optimized performance for mobile devices, ensuring responsive, polished experiences under high user load.
  • Collaborated cross-functionally with designers, artists, and QA to deliver high-quality features.
