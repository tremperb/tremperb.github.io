Kingdom Boss



About

Kingdom Boss is a mobile strategy RPG with a large global player base, featuring real-time combat, collectible heroes, and large-scale cooperative and competitive events.


My Contributions

As part of the engineering team, I focused on UI systems and VFX integration to create a polished and engaging player experience:

  • Developed and maintained menus, HUDs, and interactive dialogs with responsive, data-driven design.
  • Integrated visual effects into gameplay interfaces to enhance feedback and clarity for players.
  • Ensured performance and polish across a wide range of mobile devices.
  • Collaborated with designers, QA, and other engineers to implement maintainable and high-quality UI systems.

These efforts contributed to a seamless and visually compelling interface that supported a global player community.
